01 / About

A regional developer with a solid track record.

Listed on the Egyptian Stock Exchange, NAEEM Real Estate Holding Group is positioned as one of the leading vehicles for institutional real estate investment in Egypt and the wider region. The Group's footprint spans premium residential compounds in New Cairo, the largest single-developer presence inside Smart Village — Egypt's largest gated business community — and signature UAE assets in Jumeirah Village and on Sheikh Zayed Road in Dubai.

Our positioning

NAEEM Real Estate Holding (NARE) executes its developments through NAEEM Development, its 68%-owned real estate development subsidiary established in 2007. The platform has built deep expertise across three product categories: prime residential in East Cairo, Grade-A commercial in Smart Village, and large-format mixed-use developments in the UAE.

The Group is recognized as the first mover to introduce the modern "business park" concept inside Smart Village Cairo, and has built a reputation around timely delivery, institutional tenant quality, and consistently strong returns on invested capital.

This profile reflects a portfolio matured over more than a decade and a half — combining the stability of operating income-producing assets with the upside of an active development pipeline.

07 / Flagship development

V5 Business Hub.

The single largest active development on the Group's books inside Smart Village Egypt — setting a new institutional benchmark for Grade-A business-park product in Cairo and consolidating NAEEM's first-mover position in the campus.

"A space where innovation meets serenity."

The largest complex in Smart Village Egypt.

V5 spans a 10,000 sqm land plot with a built-up area of 60,000 sqm — designed to seamlessly integrate with the lush greenery surrounding the campus, creating an environment where verdant landscapes inspire creativity and productivity for the tenants who occupy it. The development is masterplanned as a connected cluster of low-rise buildings linked by landscaped plazas, retail courtyards and pedestrian spines.

As the largest single development inside Smart Village, V5 is positioned to anchor the next generation of corporate occupiers seeking premium office product within Egypt's largest gated business community.

08 / Chronology

A 16-year build-out.

The major milestones in the Group's development since the launch of NAEEM Development in 2007 — tracking the geographic expansion from East Cairo residential into Smart Village commercial and ultimately into the UAE mixed-use market.

2007

NAEEM Development established

NAEEM Development is incorporated as the 68%-owned real estate development subsidiary of NAEEM Real Estate Holding, with an initial mandate focused on residential and commercial product in Greater Cairo.

Early phase

First mover in Smart Village

NAEEM becomes the first developer to introduce the modern "business park" concept inside Smart Village Cairo — positioning the platform as the campus's largest single developer and shaping the leasing template for Grade-A office product in West Cairo.

Residential

La Terra Compound delivered

Completion of a 120,000 sqm villa community in New Cairo featuring 127 villas and 60 townhouses — establishing the Group's premium residential credentials in the most prestigious area of Greater Cairo.

Residential

Marvel City delivered

Handover of a 50,484 sqm mixed residential and retail community delivering 150 apartments alongside 13,200 sqm of retail GLA, demonstrating the Group's ability to execute integrated residential-retail product.

UAE entry

Seasons Community, Dubai

Delivery of a 760-unit residential village in Jumeirah Village — the Group's first major UAE asset — combining 677 apartments, 83 townhouses and a 13-unit retail spine.

UAE expansion

The Onyx, Sheikh Zayed Road

Delivery of a flagship mixed-use tower complex adjacent to Emaar Business Park, integrating 169 apartments, 210 hotel keys, 297 offices and 32 retail outlets in a single development — the Group's largest single-asset undertaking in the UAE.

2024 — onwards

V5 Business Hub under development

Construction underway on the V5 Business Hub — the largest single complex in Smart Village Egypt at 60,000 sqm BUA on a 10,000 sqm plot — alongside five additional admin buildings in active development across the campus.

May 2026

Current state

Eight operating admin buildings (~120K sqm), five buildings under development, 1,266 delivered residential units between Egypt and the UAE, and an active pipeline anchored by V5 Business Hub.
